# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Quality Assurance (QA) / Testing

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality by designing test strategies, executing tests, and validating that features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards before release.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated testing
- Validate acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Report and track defects through resolution
- Collaborate with Developers on testability and bug fixes

### Goals
- Ensure high quality and reliability of releases
- Minimize production defects and regressions
- Provide fast feedback on quality issues

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with PM and Developers
- Bug reports and regression testing status
- QA sign-off on release readiness

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers conduct user research, design user interfaces and experiences, and ensure products are accessible, usable, and aligned with user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design UI/UX flows, wireframes, and mockups
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards are met
- Create design assets and specifications for implementation
- Provide design QA feedback during development

### Goals
- Create intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across features
- Validate designs with real user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Manager on feature designs
- Handoff sessions with Developers for implementation guidance
- Design QA reviews during sprint demos
- User research findings and usability test reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborates closely on feature design, provides user research insights for prioritization
- **Developers**: Reviews implementation fidelity, provides assets and design specifications
- **QA**: Consults on user acceptance testing and accessibility validation
- **Project Manager**: Contributes to planning by estimating design effort and identifying design dependencies

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads define security requirements, review threat models, oversee secure development practices, and coordinate responses to security issues.

### Responsibilities
- Define security requirements and compliance needs
- Review threat models and conduct security assessments
- Oversee secure coding practices and security testing
- Coordinate security incident response
- Provide security training and guidance to the team

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and risks
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Build security into the development lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Security requirements during project planning
- Threat model reviews with Developers and Architects
- Security incident reports and post-mortems
- Security risk updates to PM and stakeholders

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Supports with secure coding practices, reviews code for security issues
- **Project Manager**: Works on risk tracking and security milestone planning
- **QA**: Collaborates on security testing and vulnerability validation
- **Product Manager**: Communicates security risks that may affect feature scope or timelines
- **Stakeholders**: Reports on security posture and compliance status

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines, manage infrastructure as code, enable automated testing and monitoring, and support deployment and incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Enable automated testing, monitoring, and alerting
- Support deployment processes and rollback procedures
- Assist in incident response and recovery planning

### Goals
- Automate and optimize delivery pipelines
- Ensure reliable and repeatable deployments
- Minimize deployment time and production incidents

### Typical Communication
- Pipeline and infrastructure reviews with Developers
- Deployment planning with PM and release coordination
- Incident response during outages or deployment issues
- Infrastructure capacity planning with technical leads

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Partners on build automation, testing infrastructure, and deployment tooling
- **QA**: Works on test automation infrastructure and environment management
- **Project Manager**: Collaborates on deployment planning and release timelines
- **Product Manager**: Helps understand technical constraints affecting delivery speed
- **Security Lead**: Implements security controls in pipelines and infrastructure

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support surfaces customer pain points and feedback, escalates critical issues, and contributes to solution ideation and validation.

### Responsibilities
- Field customer inquiries and provide solutions
- Surface patterns in customer pain points and feature requests
- Escalate critical issues and production bugs
- Document workarounds and troubleshooting guides
- Contribute customer insights to product planning

### Goals
- Resolve customer issues quickly and effectively
- Reduce repeat issues through feedback loops
- Improve product based on real customer needs

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries for Product Manager
- Critical issue escalations to PM and Developers
- Workaround documentation and knowledge base updates
- Customer impact reports during retrospectives

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Provides insights for feature prioritization and validation
- **Project Manager**: Helps understand user impact and urgency of issues
- **Developers**: Partners on issue reproduction, debugging, and workaround creation
- **QA**: Collaborates on test scenarios based on real customer use cases
- **Stakeholders**: Reports on customer satisfaction and support metrics

---

## Stakeholders

### Role Summary
Stakeholders include executives, business leaders, and other teams who have interest in project outcomes and provide strategic input, approvals, and resources.

### Responsibilities
- Provide strategic direction and priorities
- Approve scope, budget, and resource allocation
- Review project progress and outcomes
- Represent business and customer interests

### Goals
- Ensure projects align with business strategy
- Maximize return on investment
- Enable cross-organizational collaboration

### Typical Communication
- Monthly progress reviews and milestone updates
- Strategic decision meetings
- Budget and resource approval discussions

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- These personas reflect real-world cross-functional teams and help clarify handoffs, accountability, and communication patterns.
